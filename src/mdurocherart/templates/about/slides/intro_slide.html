Art has always had familial ties for me; My dad was a creative person as he spent many years playing guitar, he also had a background in painting with watercolours as well as sketching and numerous other techniques. Through him, as well as from his passing, I saw and understood what true passion for an art is. Growing up, my mum also encouraged my brothers as well as me to pick up art as a hobby to do in the house, as playing outside the house in the local area had come with racist encounters at the time. As a result, my brothers and me became self-taught artist, we all formed a unified passion and continue to develop our own craft. Our parents have always supported and praised us on this journey and continue to do so.
